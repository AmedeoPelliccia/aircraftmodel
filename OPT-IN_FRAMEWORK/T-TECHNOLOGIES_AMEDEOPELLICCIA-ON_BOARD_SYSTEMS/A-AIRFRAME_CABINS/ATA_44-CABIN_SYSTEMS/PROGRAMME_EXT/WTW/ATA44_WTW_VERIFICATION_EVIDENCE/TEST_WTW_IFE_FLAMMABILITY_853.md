# EVI-003 — WTW IFE LRU Flammability Test (CS-25.853)

**EVI ID:** T-A-ATA44-WTW-EVI-003
**Delta Refs:** DELTA-WTW-44-005
**Req Refs:** WTW-44-200-003
**Standard:** CS-25.853
**Status:** OPEN — Target LC07
**Parent:** [Evidence README](README.md)

---

## 1) Objective

Demonstrate that IFE LRU materials (IFE Media Server and Cabin Satellite Units) meet
CS-25.853 flammability requirements for materials installed in or accessible from the
occupied cabin.

---

## 2) Test Summary

| LRU | Material/component | Test method | Pass criterion | Target LC |
|-----|-------------------|------------|---------------|-----------|
| IFE Media Server chassis | Metallic / composite panels | CS-25.853 Appendix F | Vertical/horizontal burn as applicable | LC07 |
| CSU enclosure | Polymer housing | CS-25.853 Appendix F | 15s / 60s burn test pass | LC07 |
| CSU mounting bracket | Composite or Al | CS-25.853 Appendix F | As applicable | LC07 |
