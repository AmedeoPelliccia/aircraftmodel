# ATA 25 BWB — In-Service Damage Acceptance Limits

**Doc ID:** T-A-ATA25-BWB-ACC-004
**Variant:** BWB
**Lifecycle:** LC01 (scaffold — limits at LC04–LC05)
**Parent:** [Acceptability README](README.md)

---

## Scope

In-service damage acceptance criteria for BWB cabin equipment and furnishings.
Provides BWB-specific delta where limits differ from SHARED ATA 25 or ATA 20,
particularly for composite structure interface points.

---

## 1) Floor Grid Node / Fitting

| Damage Type | Acceptable Limit | Action if Exceeded |
|------------|-----------------|-------------------|
| Fitting wear (depth) | TBD mm | Replace fitting per CMM |
| Composite node insert crack | None acceptable | Engineering disposition |
| Parent composite panel crack at node | None acceptable | Remove from service; engineering disposition |
| Grid panel delamination (non-node zone) | TBD mm × TBD mm (non-structural skin) | CMM bonded repair |

---

## 2) Monument Damage

| Damage Type | Acceptable Limit | Action if Exceeded |
|------------|-----------------|-------------------|
| Floor attach fitting wear | TBD mm | Replace fitting; consult CMM |
| Overhead soffit attach fitting crack | None acceptable | Remove monument; engineering disposition |
| Panel crack/delamination (non-structural) | TBD mm × TBD mm maximum | Replace panel |
| Drain fitting active leak | None | Repair before next flight |
| Monument contact with spar structure | Not acceptable (see ACC_BWB_CLEARANCES_AND_ACCESS.md) | Adjust or replace |

---

## 3) Overhead Bin / Crown Soffit

| Damage Type | Acceptable Limit | Action if Exceeded |
|------------|-----------------|-------------------|
| Soffit hanger fitting wear | TBD mm play | Replace hanger |
| Soffit hanger crack | None acceptable | Remove bin; engineering disposition |
| Composite bin body dent (non-structural) | ≤TBD mm depth, no sharp edges | Blend/cosmetic repair |
| Spar cap contact (bin body) | Not acceptable | Adjust bin position |

---

## 4) Composite Structure Interface (BWB-specific escalation)

Any damage to the BWB composite floor grid structure, spar web fittings, or crown
soffit fittings **shall** be escalated to ATA 53 engineering as well as ATA 25 engineering.

Escalation path per [ATA 20 REPAIR_ACCEPTABILITY.md](../../../../ATA_20-STANDARD_PRACTICES_AIRFRAME/REPAIR_ACCEPTABILITY.md).
