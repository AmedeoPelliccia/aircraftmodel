## 1) Objective

Demonstrate CS-25.803 compliance (90-second full evacuation) and CS-25.809/810 exit slide
and lighting compliance for the BWB multi-zone radial-aisle cabin configuration.

The BWB radial egress model (fans out from each zone to nearest exit) requires a
dedicated full-scale demonstration distinct from the WTW fore-aft aisle evacuation.
