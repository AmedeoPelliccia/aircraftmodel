# TEST-EVI-003 — BWB Cabin Interior Flammability

**Evidence ID:** T-A-ATA25-BWB-EVI-003
**Type:** Test
**Delta Refs:** DELTA-BWB-25-001, DELTA-BWB-25-003, DELTA-BWB-25-004, DELTA-BWB-25-007
**CS-25 Para:** CS-25.853 + Appendix F Part I / Part II
**Status:** OPEN — target LC06
**Parent:** [Evidence README](README.md)

---

## 1) Objective

Demonstrate that all BWB cabin interior materials (seats, composite bay panels, ceiling
panels, galley inserts, lavatory panels, cargo liners) meet CS-25.853 flammability
requirements for the AMPEL360 BWB type design.

BWB-specific materials (composite-compatible adhesives, BWB crown panel systems,
composite floor panels) may differ from WTW; separate qualification records required.

---

## 2) Test Standard

| Test Method | Material Category | CS-25 Appendix |
|-------------|-----------------|----------------|
| Vertical Bunsen burner | Textiles, seat covers, small parts | Appendix F Part I (60s) |
| Horizontal Bunsen burner | Films, thin panels | Appendix F Part I (15s) |
| 45° angle test | Seat cushions | Appendix F Part I |
| Oil burner test | Seat cushions (fire blocking) | Appendix F Part II |
| Cargo liner test (Class C) | BWB lower-body cargo liners | CS-25.855 / Appendix F Part III |
