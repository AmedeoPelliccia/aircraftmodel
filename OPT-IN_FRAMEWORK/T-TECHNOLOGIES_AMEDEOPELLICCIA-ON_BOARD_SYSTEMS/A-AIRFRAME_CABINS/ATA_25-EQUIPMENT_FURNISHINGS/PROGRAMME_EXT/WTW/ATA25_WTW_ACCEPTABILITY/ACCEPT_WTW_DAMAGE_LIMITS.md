# ATA 25 WTW — In-Service Damage Acceptance Limits

**Doc ID:** T-A-ATA25-WTW-ACC-002
**Variant:** WTW
**Lifecycle:** LC01 (scaffold — limits at LC04–LC05)
**Parent:** [Acceptability README](README.md)

---

## Scope

In-service damage acceptance criteria for WTW cabin equipment and furnishings.
Provides WTW-specific delta where limits differ from SHARED ATA 25 or ATA 20.

---

## 1) Seat Damage

| Damage Type | Acceptable Limit | Action if Exceeded |
|------------|-----------------|-------------------|
| Seat track fitting wear | TBD mm wear depth | Replace fitting; consult CMM |
| Seat structure crack | None acceptable in primary load path | Remove from service; engineering disposition |
| Cushion tear/cover damage | Cosmetic only (no structural) | Cabin crew serviceable decision |
| Recline mechanism failure | N/A to structural acceptability | Cabin crew log; MCC dispatch |

---

## 2) Monument Damage

| Damage Type | Acceptable Limit | Action if Exceeded |
|------------|-----------------|-------------------|
| Panel crack/delamination | TBD mm x TBD mm maximum (non-structural) | Replace panel |
| Structural attach point crack | None acceptable | Remove monument; engineering disposition |
| Drain fitting leak | No active leak in flight | Repair before next flight |

---

## 3) Overhead Bin Damage

| Damage Type | Acceptable Limit | Action if Exceeded |
|------------|-----------------|-------------------|
| Door hinge wear | TBD mm play (functional latch) | Replace hinge |
| Structural fitting crack | None acceptable | Remove bin; engineering disposition |
| Impact dent (non-structural bin body) | ≤TBD mm depth, no sharp edges | Blend/cosmetic repair |

---

## 4) Escalation

Any damage to structural attachment points, seat tracks, or floor fittings **shall** be
escalated to engineering per [REPAIR_ACCEPTABILITY.md](../../../../ATA_20-STANDARD_PRACTICES_AIRFRAME/REPAIR_ACCEPTABILITY.md)
escalation rules.
