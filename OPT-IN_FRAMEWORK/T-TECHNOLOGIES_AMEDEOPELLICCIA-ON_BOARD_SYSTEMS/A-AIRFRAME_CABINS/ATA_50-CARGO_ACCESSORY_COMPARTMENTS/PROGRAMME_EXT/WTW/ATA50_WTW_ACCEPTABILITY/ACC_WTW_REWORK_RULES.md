# ACC — WTW ATA 50 Rework Rules

**Programme:** AMPEL360 WTW  
**ATA:** 50 Cargo & Accessory Compartments  
**Status:** TBD — rework procedures gate LC04–LC05

---

## 1. Principle

Rework rules reference ATA 20 standard practices for material, fastener
and sealant selection. This document captures only the WTW-specific
applicability and limits not covered in the SHARED baseline.

---

## 2. Liner Panel Rework

| Scenario | Rework method | Standard ref | LC gate |
|---|---|---|---|
| Seam gap exceeds limit | Re-apply Class B sealant per ATA 20 §sealant | ATA 20 standard | LC04 |
| Panel delamination at clip | Replace panel and re-torque clips | ATA 20 fastener table | LC04 |
| Panel crack at seam | Replace affected panel; inspect adjacent panels | Repair Acceptability | LC04 |
| Curved rail disbond | Remove rail, clean, re-bond per adhesive spec | ATA 20 surface treatment | LC04 |

---

## 3. Drain Mast Rework

| Scenario | Rework method | Standard ref | LC gate |
|---|---|---|---|
| Sealant disbond | Remove sealant, clean to bare metal, re-apply Class B | ATA 20 sealant table | LC04 |
| Corroded fitting | Replace mast fitting; inspect adjacent skin for pitting | ATA 20 NDT per INSPECTION_NDT.md | LC05 |

---

## 4. Cargo Door Latch Rework

| Scenario | Rework method | Standard ref | LC gate |
|---|---|---|---|
| Worn latch pin | Replace pin; verify engagement depth per installation limits | ICD-WTW-50-002 | LC04 |
| Corroded hinge pin | Replace pin; inspect bore for corrosion; apply conversion coating | ATA 20 surface treatment | LC05 |

---

## 5. Escalation

If damage exceeds rework limits in `ACC_WTW_DAMAGE_LIMITS.md`, the item
shall be escalated to engineering disposition per the mandatory escalation
trigger table in `ATA_20-STANDARD_PRACTICES_AIRFRAME/REPAIR_ACCEPTABILITY.md §4`.
