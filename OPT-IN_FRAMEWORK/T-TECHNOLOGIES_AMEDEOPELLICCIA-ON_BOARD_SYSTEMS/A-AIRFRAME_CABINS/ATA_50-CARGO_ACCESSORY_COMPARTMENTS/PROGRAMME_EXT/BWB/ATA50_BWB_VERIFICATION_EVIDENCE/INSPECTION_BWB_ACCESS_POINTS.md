# INSPECTION-BWB-50-001 — BWB Cargo Bay Access Points Inspection (CS-25.1411/CS-25.783)

**Evidence ID:** EVI-BWB-50-005
**Type:** Inspection
**Programme:** AMPEL360 BWB
**Delta:** DELTA-BWB-50-002
**Regulation:** CS-25.1411, CS-25.783
**LC Gate:** LC04
**Status:** STUB — actual inspection conducted at LC04 (first article)

## 1. Objective

Verify that BWB cargo bay side-access doors meet:
- CS-25.783 (fuselage door general requirements, including warning system integration)
- CS-25.1411 (required safety equipment access — emergency equipment accessible without tools)

## 2. Inspection Scope

- All cargo bay doors (one per bay): DOOR-BWB-C1 through DOOR-BWB-C4.
- Door clear opening dimensions vs. ICD-BWB-50-002 requirements.
- Latch mechanism engagement vs. installation limits.
- Ground lock pin operation: external actuation accessible to ground crew.
- Door warning circuit: confirm indication at flight deck and ground station.
- Emergency equipment access: confirm accessible without tools or cargo removal.

## 3. Inspection Method

- Visual inspection of door installation.
- Dimensional measurement: clear opening (width, height, sill height).
- Functional test: latch engagement, ground lock, door open/close operation.
- Electrical continuity check: door warning circuit per wiring diagram.
- Emergency equipment access walkthrough per checklist.

## 4. Acceptance Criteria

- Clear opening meets ICD-BWB-50-002 minimum dimensions (TBD at LC03).
- Latch engagement ≥ minimum per ACC_BWB_INSTALLATION_LIMITS.
- Ground lock pin engages and disengages from external position.
- Door warning indication confirmed at flight deck within TBD s of door opening.
- Emergency equipment accessible without tools from bay access door.
