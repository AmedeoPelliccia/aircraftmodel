# ANALYSIS-BWB-50-002 — BWB Cargo Restraint Analysis (CS-25.787/789/561)

**Evidence ID:** EVI-BWB-50-002
**Type:** Analysis
**Programme:** AMPEL360 BWB
**Delta:** DELTA-BWB-50-006
**Regulation:** CS-25.787, CS-25.789, CS-25.561
**LC Gate:** LC05
**Status:** STUB — actual analysis produced at LC05

## 1. Objective

Demonstrate that BWB cargo net/restraint attachment fittings and spar frame
floor nodes meet the strength requirements of CS-25.787, CS-25.789 and CS-25.561
for cargo stowage and restraint under emergency load conditions.

## 2. Scope

- Per-bay restraint net attach fitting loads under CS-25.561 inertia loads.
- Metallic insert design for composite spar frame floor nodes.
- Net ultimate strength demonstration per CS-25.787.
- Retention of items of mass per CS-25.789 under all load cases.

## 3. Method (Planned)

- FEA of spar frame floor node with metallic insert under combined load cases.
- CS-25.561 inertia factors applied to maximum cargo density per bay.
- Insert pull-out strength vs. composite spar web laminate (bearing/shear failure modes).
- Net fitting ultimate load test correlation (test stub: TBD).

## 4. Inputs Required (TBD at LC05)

- Final cargo density (kg/m²) from payload definition.
- CS-25.561 load factors confirmed by loads report.
- Metallic insert design from structures team.
- Spar web laminate properties from material qualification data.

## 5. Success Criteria

- Fitting ultimate load ≥ CS-25.561 × cargo mass × safety factor.
- No insert pull-out at DUL.
- Net fitting deformation ≤ allowable at DLL.
