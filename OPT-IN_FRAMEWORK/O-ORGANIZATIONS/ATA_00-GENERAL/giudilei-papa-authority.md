## Purpose

The **GIUDILEI PAPA** (GP) is the supreme governance authority for the
AMPEL360 programme family. This role exercises final decision-making power
in cross-programme scenarios where standard programme-level authority is
insufficient to resolve conflicts or approve changes.

The GP role is **not** a day-to-day operational role. It is activated only
under the conditions defined in §4 below.

|---|---|

| Family Programme Manager | Programme execution authority — initiates GP activation |
| Certification Authority (EASA/FAA) | External regulatory interface — GP provides internal alignment prior to external submission |
| Safety Officer | Safety case review — GP holds veto on cross-programme safety trade-offs |

---

## Activation Conditions

The GP role is activated when **any** of the following occur:

1. **IBCR Deadlock:** Two programme Chief Engineers cannot reach consensus
   on an Inheritance Boundary Change Request within the standard review cycle
2. **Certification Conflict:** A certification dispute requires escalation
   beyond the programme level (e.g., conflicting EASA AMC interpretations
   between WTW and BWB)
3. **Tokenomics Dispute:** A cross-programme TT allocation conflict arises
   that cannot be resolved by the Programme Manager
4. **Grammar (Σ) Baseline Transition:** A request to modify the structural
   grammar (ATA taxonomy, OPT-IN axes, LC schema) per GOV-3 enforcement rule
